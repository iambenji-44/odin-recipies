Description

Lasagna is a singular rectangular sheet of pasta, and it is not “noodles” (whatever that means).

Lasagne (“e” is plural feminine in Italian - with a few exceptions -, as lasagna is singular feminine)

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Fresh Herb
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
