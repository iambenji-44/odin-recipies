Cassava Flakes

Home

Description

Cassava flakes, also known as gari, are a staple food in many parts of Africa, made from the grated, fermented, and roasted cassava root. They are a good source of carbohydrates and can be used in various dishes, both sweet and savory.

Ingredients

  • Gari/Cassava Flakes
  • Sugar
  • Groundnut
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix gari with sugar and groundnut
  2. Add water to the mixture
  3. Add milk to taste
  4. Serve
