Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a breakfast food made from oats, water or milk, and sometimes other ingredients. It's a good source of fiber, vitamins, and minerals.

Ingredients

  • Oat
  • Sugar/Fruits
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil the Oat
  2. Add your desired sugar/fruits
  3. Add milk to taste
  4. Serve
